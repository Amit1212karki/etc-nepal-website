Mission

Committed in upgrading the social, organizational and technical skills of the local people and conducting research, capacity building, vocational skill training and employment activities with high professional ethics in different sectors with modern technologies to contribute to the efforts of overall nation building in a sustainable manner. Main vision is to reduce poverty of country's people by providing different types of vocational skill training.
