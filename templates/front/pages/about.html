A brief introduction of ETC.

Expert Technical College Nepal (ETC Nepal) Pvt. Ltd.is registered under company act and recognized by CTEVT to conduct the vocational skill training with a standard training curriculum. In 2071 BS, it has established its head office in Itahari, Sunsari District. It has one building with two storied with 10 rooms at its head office. It has practical labs, practical field. Its branch office has also separate rooms for all trades of training; practical lab and open field for training.

Expert Technical College Nepal (ETC) has aimed to facilitate school leavers, poor and women and marginalized youth providing them skill training with on the Job training (OJT), assisting them to generate self-employment and find employment in domestic and international labour market. Its primary objective is to produce qualified semi-skilled and skilled technical workforce as per the demand of the market placing them in real field of practice like On The Job training (OJT). The main thrusts of the company are quality service delivery, sustainability, competitiveness, innovative technologies, employability and research and development. The training, counselling, promoting, research and development are the means the company uses to mainstream and empower the target group. The school dropouts, out-of-school youths, job aspirant unskilled youths, industrial workers, youths from disadvantaged group (DAG), Dalit, women, conflict affected and internally displaced groups are our primary focus.

The major objectives of ETC are to assess and update labour market information; to explore feasibility of new trade of training; to impart training to the unemployed youths as per local and global market demand; and to support the trainees for placement i.e. wage employment and creation of self-employment. Up to now, it has been working in thirteen districts of eastern development region but it aims to work all over Koshi & Madhesh Provence of Nepal. It has been delivering its services to its targeted population from grassroots to district level for their upliftment through market oriented skills training. ETC assesses the demands of the local and global labour market and disseminate information regarding the market needs. The youths who are in search of vocational skill development for employment come in touch with ETC and they are enrolled in trades of their choices. Besides this, ETC also works through local partners i.e. NGOs, DDCs, Rural Municipalities, Municipalities, INGOs, and government line agencies. Up to now, ETC has collaborated with local governments, HELVETAS Nepal, SaMi, ENSSURE/ CTEVT, CTEVT province 1 & 2, Ministry of Social Development, Youth creation Sunsari, DDC, RSDC, Care Nepal/CSP, GIZ, Lutheran World Federation, Nepal Red Cross Society, IRC Nepal, Multi Skill, The Asia Foundation, NCDC, Karuna Foundation, EVENT/ MoE, VSDTA, Gharelutatha San Udhyog Udayapur, and Sunsari, Sahara Nepal/RMDC in conducting skill development training in various trades in different geographical location of the country.

Mission

Committed in upgrading the social, organizational and technical skills of the local people and conducting research, capacity building, vocational skill training and employment activities with high professional ethics in different sectors with modern technologies to contribute to the efforts of overall nation building in a sustainable manner. Main vision is to reduce poverty of country's people by providing different types of vocational skill training.

Vision

ETC Pvt. Ltd. emerged with a mission to deliver an expertise and professional services and inputs in the field of innovative skill enhancement training keeping view on environment friendly infrastructure and social development for the enhancement of rural livelihoods. Its main mission is to deliver quality training to poor people and make them self, wage employment.

Our Goal

  • Provide technical and vocational trainings which are focused for the direct employment
  • Provide quality training which enables the youth to compete with others in current market.
  • Increase the employability of the school dropout and unemployed youths through vocational trainings
  • Provide counselling services for unemployed youths to choose a better career path.
  • Create employment and livelihood opportunities for unemployed youths.
  • Provide counselling services for trainees willing to go abroad for foreign employment.
  • Provide trainings, workshops, seminars on various sectors of vocational trainings
  • Prepare books, manuals, notes, brochures based on the job market after the proper research.

Organizational Chart

We have a Managing Director under the Management board who will lead the completely training programs; also called Project Coordinator for any project. One Training Coordinator will depute under the Managing Director. District wise field officers will be appointed to work closely with the instructors, communities and trainees who will coordinate directly with Training Coordinator. Lead Instructors/Co- instructors will be managed insuring 2 instructors for a group of 20 trainees in a subject. Administration and Finance manager will be deputed in the centre to manage all the logistics, administration and financial activities; an administrative officer and an accountant will work under the direct supervision of the Project Coordinator. There will be placement and counselling officer, data base officer, receptionist and office assistants as supporting staff. A Life skill & Business skill Instructor will be appointed for the outreach activities, gender sensitization, and social inclusion; counselling and placement supports under the supervision Counsellors and placement support officers will also work in this unit.
