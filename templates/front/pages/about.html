A brief introduction of ETC.

Expert Technical College Nepal (ETC Nepal) Pvt. Ltd.is registered under company act and recognized by CTEVT to conduct the vocational skill training with a standard training curriculum. In 2071 BS, it has established its head office in Itahari, Sunsari District. It has one building with two storied with 10 rooms at its head office. It has practical labs, practical field. Its branch office has also separate rooms for all trades of training; practical lab and open field for training.

Expert Technical College Nepal (ETC) has aimed to facilitate school leavers, poor and women and marginalized youth providing them skill training with on the Job training (OJT), assisting them to generate self-employment and find employment in domestic and international labour market. Its primary objective is to produce qualified semi-skilled and skilled technical workforce as per the demand of the market placing them in real field of practice like On The Job training (OJT). The main thrusts of the company are quality service delivery, sustainability, competitiveness, innovative technologies, employability and research and development. The training, counselling, promoting, research and development are the means the company uses to mainstream and empower the target group. The school dropouts, out-of-school youths, job aspirant unskilled youths, industrial workers, youths from disadvantaged group (DAG), Dalit, women, conflict affected and internally displaced groups are our primary focus.

The major objectives of ETC are to assess and update labour market information; to explore feasibility of new trade of training; to impart training to the unemployed youths as per local and global market demand; and to support the trainees for placement i.e. wage employment and creation of self-employment. Up to now, it has been working in thirteen districts of eastern development region but it aims to work all over Koshi & Madhesh Provence of Nepal. It has been delivering its services to its targeted population from grassroots to district level for their upliftment through market oriented skills training. ETC assesses the demands of the local and global labour market and disseminate information regarding the market needs.
