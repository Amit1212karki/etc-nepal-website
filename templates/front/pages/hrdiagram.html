Organizational Chart

We have a Managing Director under the Management board who will lead the completely training programs; also called Project Coordinator for any project. One Training Coordinator will depute under the Managing Director. District wise field officers will be appointed to work closely with the instructors, communities and trainees who will coordinate directly with Training Coordinator. Lead Instructors/Co- instructors will be managed insuring 2 instructors for a group of 20 trainees in a subject. Administration and Finance manager will be deputed in the centre to manage all the logistics, administration and financial activities; an administrative officer and an accountant will work under the direct supervision of the Project Coordinator. There will be placement and counselling officer, data base officer, receptionist and office assistants as supporting staff. A Life skill & Business skill Instructor will be appointed for the outreach activities, gender sensitization, and social inclusion; counselling and placement supports under the supervision Counsellors and placement support officers will also work in this unit.
