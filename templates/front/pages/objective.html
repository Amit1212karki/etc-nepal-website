Objectives

1. Enhance Employment Opportunities: Provide tailored vocational and technical training programs aimed at increasing the employability of marginalized groups, including school dropouts and unemployed youths.

2. Empower Youth for Self-Sufficiency: Equip individuals with market-relevant skills to enable them to secure self-employment or wage employment, contributing to economic self-sufficiency.

3. Promote Environmentally Sustainable Practices: Incorporate environmentally friendly approaches into skill enhancement training to align with sustainable development goals and social responsibility.

4. Foster Capacity Building and Professional Development: Conduct research, workshops, seminars, and capacity-building initiatives aimed at upgrading the technical and social skills of trainees in line with modern technologies.

5. Facilitate Career Guidance and Counseling: Provide career counseling services to unemployed youths, helping them make informed decisions regarding career paths and foreign employment opportunities.

6. Develop Training Materials Aligned with Market Needs: Prepare high-quality training materials, such as manuals, notes, and brochures, based on in-depth job market research to ensure relevance and impact.

7. Contribute to Nation Building through Skilled Workforce Development: Support national development efforts by providing a skilled workforce that contributes to various sectors, thus helping reduce poverty and fostering economic growth.
